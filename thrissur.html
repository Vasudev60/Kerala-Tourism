Getting to Thrissur

Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport (COK), approximately 55 km away.

Main Railway Station: Thrissur Railway Station, located in the heart of the city.

Main Bus Station: Thrissur KSRTC Bus Stand, centrally located.

Notable Restaurants

Zam Zam Restaurant - Known for traditional Kerala cuisine.

Royal Darbar - Offers a variety of Indian and Mughlai dishes.

Arya Bhavan - Notable for its vegetarian South Indian food.

Thalappakattu Biriyani - Known for its authentic biriyani.

Royal Food Court - Offers a range of Indian and Chinese dishes.

Things to Do in Thrissur

Visit Vadakkunnathan Temple - An ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Explore Thrissur Zoo and Museum - Home to a variety of animal species and artifacts.

Witness Thrissur Pooram - A grand festival held annually with elephants, fireworks, and music.

Take a stroll in Snehatheeram Beach - Enjoy the serene ambiance and scenic beauty.
