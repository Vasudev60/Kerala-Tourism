Must Visit Destinations

Munnar

Alleppey

Alappuzha (or Alleppey) is a city on the Laccadive Sea in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It's best known for houseboat cruises along the rustic Kerala backwaters, a network of tranquil canals and lagoons.

Kovalam

Kovalam is a small coastal town in the southern Indian state of Kerala, south of Thiruvananthapuram. It's known for its palm-fringed beaches, including Lighthouse Beach, Hawa Beach, and Samudra Beach.

Varkala

Varkala, a coastal town in Kerala, India, is renowned for its stunning cliffs overlooking the Arabian Sea. It attracts travelers with its pristine beaches, atmosphere and vibrant cafes.

Fort Kochi

Fort Kochi, located in Kerala, India, is a historic coastal area known for its colonial architecture, diverse culture, and vibrant art scene. Visitors flock here to explore its picturesque streets, ancient forts, and bustling waterfront.

Wayanad

Wayanad is a picturesque district in Kerala, offering lush green landscapes, wildlife sanctuaries, and adventurous trekking routes.

Kumarakom

Kumarakom is a beautiful village in Kerala, India, famous for its backwater tourism. It's known for its serene Vembanad Lake, lush greenery, and houseboat cruises. Visitors come to relax, enjoy nature

Thrissur

Thrissur is a city in Kerala, India. It's famous for its cultural heritage, festivals like Thrissur Pooram, and temples like Vadakkunnathan Temple.

Kozhikode

Kozhikode, a city in Kerala, India, boasts historic significance, scenic beaches,delectable cuisine. It's a blend of culture, heritage, offering a vibrant South Indian experience natural beauty

Gavi

Gavi is a lovely place in Kerala full of trees and animals.People like to visit Gavi because it's calm and has pretty nature.You can see birds and maybe even elephants.It's a peaceful place for families and anyone likes being in nature.

Vagamon

Vagamon is a beautiful place in Kerala with lots of green fields, hills covered in mist, and stunning views. It's great for people who love nature.

Silent Valley National Park

Silent Valley National Park,located in the Nilgiri Hills,is a biodiversity hotspot known for its pristine rainforests,diverse wildlife,and rare plant species.
