Getting to Alleppey

Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport (COK), approximately 83 km away.

Main Railway Station: Alappuzha Railway Station, located in the city.

Main Bus Station: Alappuzha Bus Stand, centrally located for easy access.

Things to Do in Alleppey

Houseboat Cruise in Backwaters - Explore the scenic backwaters on a traditional houseboat.

Visit Alleppey Beach - Relax on the sandy shores and witness breathtaking sunsets.

Explore Alappuzha Lighthouse - Climb to the top for panoramic views of the city.

Experience Snake Boat Race - Witness the thrilling snake boat races held during Onam festival.
