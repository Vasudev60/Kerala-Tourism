Getting to Munnar

Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport (COK), approximately 110 km away.

Main Railway Station: Aluva Railway Station, approximately 110 km away.

Main Bus Station: Munnar Bus Stand, located in the heart of the town.

Things to Do in Munnar

Visit Eravikulam National Park - Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr.

Explore Mattupetty Dam - Enjoy boating and take in the scenic views.

Trek to Anamudi Peak - Highest peak in South India, perfect for adventure enthusiasts.

Take a tea plantation tour - Learn about the process of tea making in Munnar's lush tea gardens.
