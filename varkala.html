Getting to Varkala

Nearest Airport: Trivandrum International Airport (TVM), approximately 55 km away.

Main Railway Station: Varkala Sivagiri Railway Station, approximately 3 km away.

Main Bus Station: Varkala Bus Stand, located in the heart of the town.
