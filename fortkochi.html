Getting to Fort Kochi

Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport (COK), approximately 40 km away.

Main Railway Station: Cochin Harbour Railway Station, approximately 10 km away.

Main Bus Station: Kunnumpuram Bus Stand, approximately 1 km away.
