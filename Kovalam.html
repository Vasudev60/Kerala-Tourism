Kovalam

Nearest Airport: Trivandrum International Airport (TRV), approximately 15 km away.

Main Railway Station: Trivandrum Central Railway Station, located in the city.

Main Bus Station: Trivandrum Central Bus Station, well-connected to major cities.

Things to Do in Kovalam

Experience Ayurvedic massages - Rejuvenate your body and mind with traditional Ayurvedic treatments.
