Getting to Kozhikode

Nearest Airport: Calicut International Airport (CCJ), approximately 28 km away.

Main Railway Station: Kozhikode Railway Station, located in the heart of the city.

Main Bus Station: Kozhikode KSRTC Bus Stand, situated in the downtown area.

Notable Restaurants

Paragon Restaurant - Known for its authentic Malabar cuisine.

Things to Do in Kozhikode

Visit Kozhikode Beach - Enjoy the sunset and explore local street food stalls.

Discover Pazhassiraja Museum & Art Gallery - Learn about the history and culture of Kerala.

Take a walk in Mananchira Square - Experience the vibrant atmosphere and lush greenery.

Explore Beypore - Witness traditional boat-building and enjoy a boat ride in the backwaters.
