Getting to Kumarakom

Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport (COK), approximately 110 km away.

Main Railway Station: Aluva Railway Station, approximately 110 km away.

Main Bus Station: Kumarakom Bus Stand, located in the heart of the town.

Things to Do in Kumarakom

Visit Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary - Home to a variety of bird species.

Explore Vembanad Lake - Enjoy boating and witness the scenic beauty.

Experience Ayurvedic Treatments - Rejuvenate with traditional Ayurvedic therapies.

Take a backwater cruise - Enjoy the serene backwaters of Kumarakom on a houseboat.
