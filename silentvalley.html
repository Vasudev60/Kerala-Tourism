Things to do in Silent Valley

Attapaddi Hills - A home for majestic peaks and hills.

Shanti Ayurveda Ashram - Wellness center specialized in Ayurvedic treatment and cure.

Keralamkundu Waterfall - Crystal clear water and beautiful landscape.

Hiking, Trekking, and Camping and exploring the wildlife through the rainfed forest.
